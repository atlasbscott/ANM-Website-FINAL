Founder & Director

Atlas Scott

Founder, Creative Director & Lead Camera Operator

Atlas Narrative Media was born from a simple belief: that every brand, artist, and property deserves to be seen — not just captured. We exist at the intersection of technical mastery and storytelling instinct, where a single frame can shift how someone sees the world.

With roots in cinematography and a career spanning corporate boardrooms, listing photography, artist studios, and indie film sets, I built ANM to be the creative partner I always wished existed — one that listens first, frames second, and never stops until the story lands.

Every project we take on is a collaboration. Your vision is the foundation. Our craft is what makes it impossible to ignore.

How We Work

From concept to final delivery

01

Consult

We start by listening. A discovery call to understand your goals, audience, timeline, and vision — before any camera leaves the bag.

02

Pre-Production

Shot lists, mood boards, location scouting, scheduling. Every detail locked in before day one so nothing is left to chance on set.

03

Production

Cameras roll. We execute with precision and stay adaptive — capturing what was planned and the moments that weren't.

04

Files optimized for every platform, revision rounds, and a final handoff you can publish with confidence. You're never left figuring it out alone.
